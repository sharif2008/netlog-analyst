Dataset

CICIDS2017 Dataset

Description: A comprehensive dataset for network intrusion detection containing both normal and attack traffic patterns. Unlike traditional synthetic datasets, CIC-IDS-2017 incorporates up-to-date intrusion techniques such as DDoS, Brute Force, Botnet, Infiltration, Web Attacks, and Port Scanning.

Source: Canadian Institute for Cybersecurity (CIC)

Citation:

Sharafaldin, I., Lashkari, A. H., & Ghorbani, A. A. (2018). Toward generating a new intrusion detection dataset and intrusion traffic characterization. In Proceedings of the 4th International Conference on Information Systems Security and Privacy (ICISSP) (pp. 108-116).
